The Case

CEO Minseo's
Empire Is Crumbling

Minseo is the chairman of Top K-Pop Entertainment and the producer of the show. A legendary figure in the K-Pop industry, he rose to fame in the late 1990s with a groundbreaking boy group and became an emblem of the Hallyu wave — propelling Korean pop culture onto the global stage.

Now, with 71 million subscribers watching and a historic debut on the line, someone on the inside has sabotaged everything. Costumes destroyed. Choreography files corrupted. Catering poisoned. Six suspects. Four weeks. One truth. Your mission: use AI to uncover the saboteur before the final reveal.

How the Game Works

Designed for elementary through 8th grade teachers. No singing or dancing required — this is a professional development experience that teaches AI tools through an immersive K-Pop mystery narrative.

01 📋

Register Your Team

Sign up as a school team. Each team gets a trainee name, group tagline, and access to the investigation portal.

02 🔓

Weekly Mission Drops

Every Monday a new Treasure Trove unlocks. Clues, tools, and mission details are released on a schedule you control.

03 🤖

Submit & Get AI Scored

Upload your artifacts directly to the TopKpop.io portal. No social media account required. The Oracle of the Rubric evaluates your work and gives you formative feedback so you can revise before final scoring.

04 🔍

Accuse the Saboteur

On the final day, submit your accusation. The team with the highest score AND the correct culprit wins the investigation.
