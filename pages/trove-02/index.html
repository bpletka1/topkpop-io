Choose Your Path  ·  Three Options

Option 1 — Rewrite Lyrics Only — Up to 90 pts Option 2 — Suno AI Song — Up to 100 pts Option 3 — Karaoke Video — Up to 100 pts + School Bonus Prize

Option 1  ·  Rewrite a Multilingual Song (Up to 90 Points)

  • 1
    Choose your song, theme, and vocabulary. Select a cover song you enjoy (K-pop, Latin pop, holiday classic, etc.). Choose a theme (identity, courage, belonging, etc.) and select 7–10 vocabulary words in Korean or Spanish.
  • 2
    Find the original lyrics. Use Google ("Song Title lyrics") or AZLyrics. Copy the full lyrics into a document.
  • 3
    Use the Song Rewriter GPT above. Paste in the original lyrics, your vocabulary list, and your theme. The GPT will rewrite the song keeping the melody and rhythm while weaving in your vocabulary meaningfully.
  • 4
    Review and refine your draft. Check that the lyrics fit the tone, use vocabulary correctly, match the song's flow, and express your theme. Ask the GPT to adjust as needed.
  • 5
    Score with the Practice Oracle, then submit officially below.

Option 2  ·  Turn Your Lyrics Into a Suno AI Song (Up to 100 Points)

  • 1
    Complete Steps 1–4 from Option 1 to create your rewritten multilingual lyrics.
  • 2
    Open Suno AI. Paste your rewritten lyrics directly into Suno's lyrics box.
  • 3
    Describe your musical style. Examples: "Upbeat K-pop dance track," "Bright Latin pop celebration," "Emotional ballad with a modern sound." Do NOT reference real artists by name.
  • 4
    Generate your AI song. Click Share and copy your Suno song link. Include the link in your submission below.

Option 3  ·  Create an AI Karaoke Video (Up to 100 Points + School Bonus Prize)

  • 1
    Complete Steps 1–4 from Option 1 to create your rewritten multilingual lyrics.
  • 2
    Open MyKaraoke.Video. Upload your legally purchased song file (mp3, m4a). The tool removes the original vocals automatically.
  • 3
    Upload your rewritten lyrics. Sync them with the timing of the music. Adjust formatting and pacing as needed.
  • 4
    Export your karaoke video and submit the file or link through the portal below. Option 3 qualifies your school for a bonus prize.

✓ Bonus Mission (+25 pts): Perform your song or have your AI avatar perform it. Submit the performance video with your main submission.
