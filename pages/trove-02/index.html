▶ Option 1  ·  Rewrite a Multilingual Song (Up to 90 Points)

How to Rewrite Your Multilingual Song

  • 1
    Choose your song, theme, and vocabulary. Select a cover song you enjoy — K-pop, Latin pop, holiday classic, or any song you know well. Choose a theme (identity, courage, belonging, etc.) and select 7–10 vocabulary words in Korean or Spanish that connect to your theme.
  • 2
    Find the original lyrics. Use Google ("Song Title lyrics") or AZLyrics.com. Copy the full lyrics into a Google Doc or text document so you can work with them.
  • 3
    Open the Song Rewriter GPT. Click the purple button above. Paste in: (1) the original lyrics, (2) your vocabulary list with translations, and (3) your theme. The GPT will rewrite the song keeping the melody and rhythm while weaving in your vocabulary meaningfully.
  • 4
    Review and refine your draft. Check that the lyrics fit the song's tone and rhythm, vocabulary is used correctly and meaningfully, the theme comes through clearly, and the song still flows when sung. Ask the GPT to adjust any lines that feel off.
  • 5
    Score with the Practice Oracle (below), then submit officially. Use the Practice Oracle as many times as you need before your final submission.

Example Prompt for Song Rewriter GPT

"Here are the original lyrics to [Song Title] by [Artist]. My theme is [theme]. My vocabulary list is: [word 1 — translation], [word 2 — translation], [word 3 — translation]... Please rewrite this song keeping the same melody and rhythm, weaving in my vocabulary words meaningfully. Keep the same number of lines per verse and chorus."

▶ Option 2  ·  Turn Your Lyrics Into a Suno AI Song (Up to 100 Points)

How to Create Your Suno AI Evidence Song

  • 1
    Complete all steps from Option 1 first. You need finished multilingual lyrics before you can generate a Suno song. Do not skip the lyric-writing step.
  • 2
    Open Suno AI (suno.com). Create a free account if you do not have one. Click "Create" and select "Custom Mode" so you can paste your own lyrics.
  • 3
    Paste your rewritten lyrics directly into Suno's lyrics box. Do not use the auto-generate lyrics option — your lyrics must be the ones you wrote.
  • 4
    Describe your musical style. In the style prompt, write something like: "Upbeat K-pop dance track," "Bright Latin pop with a modern sound," or "Emotional ballad with synth elements." Do NOT reference real artists by name — Suno will reject those prompts.
  • 5
    Generate your AI song. Suno will create two versions. Listen to both and choose the one that best fits your lyrics and theme. You can regenerate as many times as you like.
  • 6
    Copy your Suno song link. Click Share on your chosen song and copy the public link. You will paste this link in your submission below.

▶ Option 3  ·  Create an AI Karaoke Video (Up to 100 Points + School Bonus Prize)

How to Create Your AI Karaoke Video

  • 1
    Complete all steps from Option 1 first. You need finished multilingual lyrics before creating the karaoke video.
  • 2
    Open MyKaraoke.Video. Upload your legally purchased song file (mp3 or m4a). The tool automatically removes the original vocals, leaving just the instrumental track.
  • 3
    Upload your rewritten lyrics. Sync them with the timing of the music. Adjust the formatting, font, and pacing so the lyrics appear on screen in time with the beat.
  • 4
    Export your karaoke video. Download the finished video file. This is what you will submit through the portal below.
  • 5
    Submit your video file or upload link below. Option 3 qualifies your school for a special bonus prize in addition to your score.
