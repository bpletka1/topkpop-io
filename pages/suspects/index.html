The Suspects.

Six people. Six motives. One saboteur. Use AI to find the truth.

The Dossiers

Everyone Had a Reason.

Each of the six suspects had access, opportunity, and — most critically — a motive. Your mission as a Detective Recruit is to analyze their backgrounds using AI tools, identify inconsistencies in their stories, and build a case that points to the true saboteur. Study each dossier carefully. The truth is in the details.

Suspect 01

Sunny Anna

Host of the Show

Known Motive

Sunny Anna believes she should have won the 100 million won prize last season. She was passed over for a newer, younger host — and she has not forgotten it. Her smile hides a competitive fire that burns hotter than any stage light.

⚠ Primary Suspect

Suspect 02

James Paul

Master Makeup Artist

Known Motive

Swedish-Korean heritage and a portfolio of K-beauty secrets that could shake the industry. James Paul guards his techniques fiercely — and someone recently threatened to expose his past. His access to every performer backstage is unmatched.

⚠ Primary Suspect

Suspect 03

Nari

Assistant to Sunny Anna

Known Motive

Nari holds an IOU for $30,000 from someone connected to the debut. She has always dreamed of becoming a K-Pop producer — and she has been quietly building connections behind the scenes. How far would she go to make that dream real?

⚠ Primary Suspect

Suspect 04

Emilise

The Choreographer

Known Motive

Half Korean, half Latina — Emilise poured her soul into the E2U choreography that made the group famous. But her credit was stolen. Her name was removed from the official release. She filed a complaint. It was ignored. She did not forget.

⚠ Primary Suspect

Suspect 05

Star 별 Tammy

Fashion Designer

Known Motive

Paris-trained and Seoul-sharp, Star Tammy designed every costume for the sabotaged debut. But three of her original designs were plagiarized by a rival label — and she suspects someone inside Top K-Pop Entertainment leaked them. Her trust is broken.

⚠ Primary Suspect

Suspect 06

Hanbit Phil

Company Chef

Known Motive

CEO Minseo's own son — hired not for his credentials but for his bloodline, or so the rumors say. Accused of nepotism by the entire staff, Hanbit Phil has spent years trying to prove himself. But the food at the pre-debut dinner made three performers ill. Coincidence?

⚠ Primary Suspect
