Mission Overview

Begin by creating your own personal K-Pop trainee identity. Decide on your style, look, and personality as an idol. You may upload a photo of yourself to transform into an idol, or describe the features, outfit, colors, and vibe you want. Choose whether your avatar looks realistic, cartoon-like, anime-inspired, or even part of a fantasy K-Pop world like demon hunters.

Once finished, save and download your avatar to use in your poster. You will post this to Instagram and introduce your character. Follow @topkpopio on Instagram — your post will be reviewed by Top K-Pop executives and accepted.

Task 1  ·  Part 1.a — How to Make Your Trainee Avatar with AI

Use ChatGPT (or Gemini) to create your K-Pop trainee avatar. This is your personal idol character for the entire game.

Step 1: Decide on Your Idol Identity

Think about who you want to be as a K-Pop trainee. Choose your style: cute, fierce, elegant, noir, cyberpunk, demon-hunter, glam, or realistic.

Step 2: Choose How You Will Make Your Avatar

Pick ONE of the following methods:

1. Upload a photo of yourself to turn into an idol
2. Describe your appearance (hair, skin tone, ethnicity, clothing, colors, vibe)
3. Describe a themed version (anime idol, K-Pop demon hunter, gemstone glam, neon street idol)

Step 3: Tell ChatGPT to Create Your Avatar

Use a sentence like:
"ChatGPT, create my trainee avatar. I want a [style] idol with [hair], [clothing], [colors], and a [vibe]."
Or, if you upload your photo, say:
"ChatGPT, turn this photo of me into a K-Pop idol version of myself."

Step 4: Review the First Version

Look at your avatar and decide if you want changes. You can ask ChatGPT to adjust: hair — lighting — outfit — pose — expression — style (realistic, anime, illustrated, dramatic, etc.)

Step 5: Finalize Your Trainee Avatar

Once you are happy with how you look, save this final version. This avatar will be used in your K-Pop Idol Poster.

Task 2  ·  Part 2.a — How to Make Your K-Pop Idol Poster

After completing your trainee avatar, create your K-Pop Idol Group Poster using AI. This is your team's official debut announcement image.

After completing your trainee avatar, create your K-Pop Idol Poster using your favorite AI (e.g. ChatGPT, Gemini). Choose or upload a dramatic background location — such as a beach, neon street, theater, or famous landmark — and place your avatar into the scene. If you are working with a team, include up to four avatars total. Add concert lights, neon effects, sparkles, and a cinematic title to complete the look. Your final poster should showcase your idol character in a high-energy K-Pop performance moment.

Step 1: Choose Your Poster Background

Select a dramatic or meaningful location for your poster. You may upload a real photo (beach, backyard, living room, school hallway), a famous landmark (Eiffel Tower, pyramids, Seoul street), or ask ChatGPT to generate a background for you.

Step 2: Upload Your Required Avatar(s)

Upload your own trainee avatar. If you are in a team, also upload every team member's avatar so all can be included in the same group poster. If you are working individually, you may upload any additional characters you want to include in your idol group (up to 4 total).

Step 3: Tell ChatGPT Your Poster Style

Describe the vibe you want for your poster — neon concert energy, glamorous comeback stage, fierce detective mood, beach performance, fantasy K-Pop theme, etc. ChatGPT will blend your characters naturally into the scene using K-Pop lighting, sparkles, beams, and stage effects.

Step 4: Add Your Title, Tagline, and Group Name

Make sure your poster has a title, a short tagline, and your idol group name so they can be added in cinematic metallic text. The tagline must include Spanish or Korean phrasing.

Step 5: Review and Approve Your Final Poster

Look at the poster ChatGPT generates and request any changes you want — lighting, color, positioning, expressions, or effects. Once you are satisfied, save your final K-Pop Idol Poster. Please feel free to pre-score your avatar and poster using the Oracle of the Rubric before submitting. Make any modifications prior to your final submission.
