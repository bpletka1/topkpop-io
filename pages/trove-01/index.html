Mission 01  ·  This Is Your Mission If You Choose to Accept It

Watch the full mission briefing below. Your assignment: create your K-Pop trainee identity using AI tools — avatar, poster, and team name — before the 10th Anniversary Season live broadcast begins.

Scoring Options

Avatar Only — Up to 75 pts Avatar + Poster — Up to 100 pts Bonus: Team Name Lore — +25 pts

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • 1
    Choose Your AI Avatar Tool. Use an AI image generator such as Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, DALL·E, or Canva AI to create your K-Pop trainee avatar. Your avatar should reflect your team's identity — style, color palette, and personality.
  • 2
    Craft Your Avatar Prompt. Write a detailed prompt describing your character's appearance, outfit, background, and mood. Be specific — the more detail you give the AI, the stronger the result.

Example Avatar Prompt

"A confident K-Pop trainee in a sleek black and gold stage outfit, standing in a neon-lit Seoul practice room. Short dark hair with highlights, intense expression, cinematic lighting, ultra-detailed digital art style."
  • 3
    Generate and Refine. Run your prompt and review the result. Adjust the prompt to improve details — lighting, expression, outfit, background. Generate at least 3 variations before choosing your final avatar.
  • 4
    Create Your K-Pop Poster (for full points). Use Canva, Adobe Express, or another design tool to create a promotional poster for your team. Include your avatar, team name, and a tagline. The poster should feel like a real K-Pop debut announcement.
  • 5
    Name Your Team. Choose a K-Pop group name for your team. For bonus points, write a short origin story (2–3 sentences) explaining the meaning behind the name and how it connects to your team's identity.
  • 6
    Score with the Practice Oracle before submitting. Paste your avatar description, poster concept, and team name into the Practice Oracle to receive coaching feedback. Revise your work, then submit officially below.
