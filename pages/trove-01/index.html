Your Mission — Step by Step

  • 1
    Use an AI image generator (ChatGPT, OpenArt, or Midjourney) to create your K-Pop idol avatar — a cinematic portrait that captures your trainee persona's style, energy, and visual identity.
  • 2
    Design an AI-generated promotional poster for your idol group. Include your trainee name, idol skills, and a group tagline written in both Korean and Spanish.
  • 3
    Submit your avatar and poster through the TopKpop.io portal. The Oracle of the Rubric will evaluate your work and provide formative feedback before your final score is recorded.
  • 4
    Study the clues embedded in the Treasure Trove briefing. They will be essential for your Final Accusation.

"ChatGPT, design a promotional poster for my K-Pop idol group called [Group Name]. Include our group tagline in Korean: [tagline] and Spanish: [tagline]. The style should be [dark neon / bright pastel / cinematic noir]. Include our trainee names and debut date."
