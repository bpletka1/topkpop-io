Mission Briefings

Three Troves. One Truth.

Each Monday morning, a new Treasure Trove unlocks — releasing the full mission details, AI tools, and embedded clues. Complete all three missions to earn your maximum score and qualify to make your Final Accusation. The Treasure Troves are not just assignments — they are pieces of evidence. Every artifact you create contains a clue.

Treasure Trove 01

Create Your K-Pop Identity

"Before you can investigate the sabotage, you must become part of the world. Build your trainee identity. The industry is watching — and so is the saboteur."

  • 1
    Use an AI image generator to create your K-Pop idol avatar — a cinematic portrait that captures your trainee persona's style, energy, and visual identity.
  • 2
    Design an AI-generated promotional poster for your idol group. Include your trainee name, idol skills, and a group tagline written in both Korean and Spanish.
  • 3
    Submit your avatar and poster through the portal. Your AI rubric score will be calculated automatically based on visual quality, cultural authenticity, and required elements.
  • 4
    Study the clues embedded in the Treasure Trove briefing. They will be essential for your Final Accusation.
Avatar — Rubric Scored Poster — Rubric Scored Early Bonus: +100 pts

Treasure Trove 02

Produce the Evidence Song

"The truth is hidden in the music. Write the song that only a true Detective Recruit could write — one that weaves the clues into every verse, every chorus, every beat."

  • 1
    Write multilingual song lyrics in English, Korean, and Spanish. The lyrics must weave in the clues collected from Treasure Trove 01 and the investigation briefings.
  • 2
    Use an AI music generation tool to produce a complete track for your song. The musical style must reflect K-Pop genre conventions.
  • 3
    Create a music video — either AI-generated visuals or a recorded performance — that brings the song to life.
  • 4
    Submit your lyrics, audio track, and video link through the portal for AI rubric scoring.
Lyrics — Up to 30 pts Music Track — Up to 30 pts Music Video — Up to 30 pts

Treasure Trove 03

Teach the Case — AI Lesson Plan

"The best investigators don't just solve cases — they teach others how to think. Design a lesson that brings the investigation into the classroom and proves you have mastered the AI tools."

  • 1
    Unpack an ELA standard and identify a sub-skill. Design an AI-integrated lesson plan that connects K-Pop culture to classroom learning for K-8 students.
  • 2
    The lesson must incorporate Korean and Spanish language elements, reference the investigation narrative, and include at least one AI tool students will use.
  • 3
    Your team performs a cultural dance that connects to the lesson's cultural theme. Submit a video link of the performance.
  • 4
    Submit your lesson plan document and performance video for AI rubric scoring. The final clues to the saboteur's identity are embedded in this Treasure Trove.
Lesson Plan — Up to 50 pts Performance — Up to 50 pts Final Clues Unlocked
