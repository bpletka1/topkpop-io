Days 29–32

The Final Accusation.

After completing all three Treasure Troves, teams collaborate, compare notes, and submit their Final Accusation. Name the saboteur. Back it up with evidence from all three Troves. The team with the highest score AND the correct culprit wins the investigation and saves the Top K-Pop 10th Anniversary Season.

Days 29–31

Collaboration Window

Teams compare clues, review evidence, and prepare their final accusation. Discuss with peers, revisit the suspect dossiers, and build your case.

Day 32

Final Accusation Due

Submit your Final Accusation through the portal. Name the saboteur and provide your evidence. Only teams who correctly identify the culprit can win.

Day 32+

The Reveal

The saboteur is unmasked. The leaderboard is finalized. The team with the highest score AND the correct accusation wins.
